    My thesis - Moodle plugin
    Installation guide :

    1. Download cptgame.rar
    2. Extract to moodle folder moodle/mod
    3. Open moodle , login as admin
    4. Choose "Upgrade Moodle database " as in this picture
    5. The following message should appear , press continue
    6. Turn Editing ON ,create a new Activity for the Course of your preference
    7. Select cptgame from the dropdown menu , enter a name then press Save and display , example here
    8. The activity will have the following form
    9. The first time you run the activity , it will create its own seperate database , required for the application. It will then redirect you to the home page of moodle
    10. Go to the activity menu , press the button and the activity will open in a new tab , the installion has successfully finished!

    How it works :
    This plugin provides an online platform for creating competitive quiz game in moodle's enviroment . There are different roles for each user , based on their role in moodle. If the user in moodle has the role of the teacher in a specific lesson he can create , review , edit Questions based on different difficulties . The quiz can have the form of multiple-choice or typing in a textbox .
    The role of the student is to play each category of questions and try to achieve the highest possible score . In order to unlock a more difficult level the student should get a passing grade on the current level he or she is in. The passing score is determined by the teacher . There are statistics for the students that provide information about their performance compared to other students .
